About Our Software

GovTrack.AI, a cutting-edge software revolutionizing the way the Press Information Bureau (PIB) interacts with media narratives. GovTrack.AI is an advanced, AI-powered platform meticulously designed to offer real-time insights and efficient feedback on government policies, initiatives, and public sentiment across diverse regional languages and media channels.

SOME KEY FEATURES OF OUR SOFTWARE

Sentiment Analysis and Categorization

Utilizing state-of-the-art Natural Language Processing (NLP), GovTrack.AI categorizes news stories into favorable (positive), neutral, or not favorable (negative) sentiments concerning the Government of India.

Intuitive Dashboard and Sorting

The user-friendly dashboard allows easy navigation and sorting of news clippings and video segments based on tonality, edition, and other relevant parameters, providing a comprehensive view of media coverage.

Multi-Lingual Media Monitoring

GovTrack.AI comprehensively monitors over 200 regional media websites, analyzing news articles in various Indian languages such as Hindi, Urdu, Punjabi, Gujarati, Marathi, Telugu, and more.

Effortless E-Paper Scanning

The software employs Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to scan and extract relevant news clippings from e-papers, simplifying the tracking of news articles pertaining to the government.

Video Segment Analysis

GovTrack.AI efficiently processes video content, utilizing closed captioning or audio-to-text features to identify and categorize news segments related to the Government of India.

Real-Time Notifications

For negative news stories, the system triggers instant notifications to the concerned PIB officers, enabling prompt responses and proactive engagement with media narratives.
